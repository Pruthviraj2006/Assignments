Information about Ratan Tata 

 
Ratan Naval Tata, known for being the former chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group. He played a crucial role in expanding the group into diverse
 sectors, including steel, automobiles, information technology, and hospitality. Under his leadership from 1991 to 2012, the Tata Group grew significantly, with a focus on
innovation and corporate social responsibility. Ratan Tata is also recognized for his commitment to philanthropy and ethical business practices.
  1. Birth: Born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India
  2. Education: Studied at the University of Mumbai and earned an MBA from Harvard Business School
  • Tata Group Leadership: Served as chairman of Tata Sons from 1991 to 2012
  • Tata Nano: Introduced the Tata Nano, aimed at providing an affordable vehicle for the masses

Ratan Tata Education


Early Education: Attended St.Xavier's High School in Mumbai.
