A state in India

Rajasthan

Rajasthan, located in the northwest of India, is the largest state by area. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and majestic forts, Rajasthan was home to the Rajput Kings. Major cities include Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur, each rich in royal heritage. It's main attraction is the Thar desert, its also known for it's vibrant culture, colorful festivals, and traditional cuisine.
