LAMBORGINI

Automobile Lamborghini S.p.A (Italian:[auto mobile Lamborghini ])is an Italian manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs based in sant'Agata Bolognese. The company is owned by the Volkswagen group through is subsidiary Audi. Ferruccio Lamborghini (1916-1993) an Italian manufacturing magnate, Lamborghini s.p.a in 1963 to compete with Ferrari.
